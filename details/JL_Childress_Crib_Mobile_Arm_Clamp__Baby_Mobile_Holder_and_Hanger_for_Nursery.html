J.L. Childress Crib Mobile Arm Clamp

This crib mobile clamp is designed to safely and securely attach baby mobiles to cribs, ensuring both functionality and protection for your baby's nursery setup. Built with padded clamps and large, soft rubber pads, it guards against scratches, dents, and wear on crib railings while offering a stable hold for your mobile.

Its compact assembly height of 18 inches keeps the mobile at an ideal, observable distance from your baby, fostering a sense of calm and comfort during sleep. This versatile device is easy to install and remove, making it perfect for use across different nursery environments or when traveling with your baby.

The foldable and lightweight design enhances portability, allowing you to take it with you wherever you go. Whether you're setting up a new nursery or looking for a durable, adjustable mobile holder for your little one, this product is a must-have for parents who value safety and style in their child's space.
